A Father's Dream.

In 2022, our journey began with a father's dream. Vivek, one of our co-founders, envisioned creating lasting memories for his newborn son. Teaming up with Anand, another co-founder who shares a deep passion for storytelling, they combined their expertise in Artificial Intelligence to develop a groundbreaking technique: seamlessly integrating real people into illustrated books..

Personalized Stories, Lasting Memories.

At Imagitime, we believe in the power of personalization. For children, our storybooks nurture imagination, creativity, and a love for reading. For adults, our tribute books capture relationships and celebrate life's meaningful connections. Using advanced AI technology, we transform photos into stunning illustrations that maintain an uncanny resemblance to your loved ones.
